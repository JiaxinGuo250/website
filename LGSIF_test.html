Laurier Graduate Student Investment Fund

This website is to show the performance and updated news of Laurier Graduate Student Investment Fund.

Laurier Graduate Student Investment Fund (LGSIF) is a student-run Investment fund following a prudent investment strategy in small cap value stocks. Graduate students are hired as analysts or portfolio managers to manage LGSIF for one year under faculty supervision and external advice from the board.
